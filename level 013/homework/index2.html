Eating healthy is essential for maintaining overall well-being. Here are some nutritious food choices to consider:

  1. Fruits and Vegetables
    • Rich in vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants.
    • Include a variety of colors to ensure a diverse nutrient intake.
    • Examples: spinach, kale, berries, oranges, and carrots.
  2. Whole Grains
    • High in fiber, which aids digestion and promotes satiety.
    • Provide sustained energy throughout the day.
    • Examples: quinoa, brown rice, oats, and whole wheat bread.
  3. Lean Proteins
    • Build and repair tissues, support immune function.
    • Opt for lean cuts of meat, poultry, fish, or plant-based proteins.
    • Examples: chicken breast, salmon, tofu, beans, and lentils.
  4. Healthy Fats
    • Essential for brain function and hormone production.
    • Include sources of omega-3 fatty acids and monounsaturated fats.
    • Examples: avocado, nuts (like almonds and walnuts), olive oil, and flaxseeds.
